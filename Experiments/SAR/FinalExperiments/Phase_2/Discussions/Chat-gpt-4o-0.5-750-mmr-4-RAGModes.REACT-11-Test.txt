### Iterative Discussion

**Domain Expert (DE):** Sure, we need to capture incidents and impacts, weather conditions, data from various sources, and missions and their relationships. For incidents and impacts, we'll need concepts like "WildfireIncident," "Impact," "Priority," and "Urgency."

**Knowledge Worker (KW):** Don't forget about the weather conditions. We need "Temperature," "WindSpeed," "Humidity," and "WeatherForecast." These are critical for understanding the fire's behavior.

**KE:** Agreed. Let's also include data from human and earth observations. We'll need concepts like "SensorData," "SatelliteData," "SocialMediaData," and properties to link them to their sources and timestamps.

**DE:** For missions and relationships between services, we need "Mission," "Service," "Support," and "Location." We should also capture the relationships between these entities.
