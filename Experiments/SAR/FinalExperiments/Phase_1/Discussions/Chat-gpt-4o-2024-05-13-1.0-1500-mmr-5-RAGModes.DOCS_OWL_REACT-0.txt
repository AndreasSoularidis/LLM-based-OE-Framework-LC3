### Iterative Discussion

#### Knowledge Engineer (KE), Domain Expert (DE), and Knowledge Worker (KW) engage in a collaborative discussion to build the ontology:
#### **Round 1: Initial Setup**

KE: I will start by defining the basic structure of our ontology. We need classes for 'Incident', 'WeatherCondition', 'ObservationData', 'Mission', and 'Service'. We will connect them appropriately through properties.

DE: Yes, "Incident" should include details like incident location, priority, impact, etc. "WeatherCondition" should capture variables like temperature, wind speed, humidity, and forecasts.

KW: And don't forget properties for "ObservationData" from sensors, satellites, and social media, as well as links between these classes.

KE: Let me start by adding these initial classes and properties to our ontology.

```ttl
@prefix : <http://example.com/sar#> .
@prefix owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#> .
@prefix rdfs: <http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#> .
@prefix xsd: <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#> .

:Incident rdf:type owl:Class .
:WeatherCondition rdf:type owl:Class .
:ObservationData rdf:type owl:Class .
:Mission rdf:type owl:Class .
:Service rdf:type owl:Class .

:hasPriority rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ; 
             rdfs:domain :Incident ;
             rdfs:range xsd:string .

:hasLocation rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
             rdfs:domain :Incident ;
             rdfs:range xsd:string .

:hasImpact rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
           rdfs:domain :Incident ;
           rdfs:range xsd:string .

:hasTemperature rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty ;
                rdfs:domain :WeatherCondition ;
                rdfs:range xsd:float .

:hasWindSpeed rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty ;
              rdfs:domain :WeatherCondition ;
              rdfs:range xsd:float .

:hasHumidity rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty ;
             rdfs:domain :WeatherCondition ;
             rdfs:range xsd:float .

:hasForecast rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty ;
             rdfs:domain :WeatherCondition ;
             rdfs:range xsd:string .

:hasObservationData rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
                    rdfs:domain :Incident ;
                    rdfs:range :ObservationData .

:cameFromSensor rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
                rdfs:domain :ObservationData ;
                rdfs:range xsd:string .

:cameFromSatellite rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;

                 rdfs:domain :Mission ;
                 rdfs:range xsd:string .

:serviceType rdf:type owl:DatatypeProperty ;
             rdfs:domain :Service ;
             rdfs:range xsd:string .

```

#### **Round 2: Further Detailing**

KE: Now let’s refine the ontology by including more specific elements, like wildfire forms and vulnerable objects involved.

DE: For "Incident", let's specify forms such as 'Creeping', 'Vertical', and 'Mixed'.

KW: How about adding data collection dates and locations for "ObservationData"?

KE: That’s a good idea. I will add these details next.

```ttl

:WildfireForm rdf:type owl:Class .
:Creeping rdf:type :WildfireForm .
:Vertical rdf:type :WildfireForm .
:Mixed rdf:type :WildfireForm .

:involvesForm rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
              rdfs:domain :Incident ;
              rdfs:range :WildfireForm .

:Population rdf:type :VulnerableObject .
:Livestock rdf:type :VulnerableObject .
:Forest rdf:type :VulnerableObject .

:hasVulnerableObject rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
                     rdfs:domain :Incident ;
                     rdfs:range :VulnerableObject .

                 rdfs:domain :ObservationData ;
                 rdfs:range xsd:dateTime .

:observationLocation rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;
                     rdfs:domain :ObservationData ;
                     rdfs:range xsd:string . 
```

#### **Round 3: Answer Competency Questions**

KE: Let’s make sure our ontology can answer the competency questions. We need properties to relate "WeatherCondition" directly to both "Incident" and "ObservationData".

DE: Add data properties to capture these key variables directly.

KW: Great, let's also include support services and population details for “Mission”.

```ttl
:currentMeasurements rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;

:missionSupport rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty ;

                rdfs:range :Service .

```
